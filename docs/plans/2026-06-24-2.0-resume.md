## 2.0 gating model (target)
- Base (paid $2.99 download): `default`, `math`, `math2` + free themes + all free UX features → never locked.
- À la carte $1.99 (non-consumable): finance, symbols, programmer, units, scientific, datetime, business, international, programmerPlus → locked until that product owned, OR Pro/grandfathered.
- Pro-only: `custom` (custom-keys pack), premium themes, customizable keyboard, iCloud sync.
- Pro $11.99 (`numpad.pro.lifetime`) unlocks everything + future. Early-bird `numpad.pro.lifetime.earlybird` (~$5.99) grants identical Pro.
