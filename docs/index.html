Privacy Policy

More Voltage does not sell or share your data and does not track you across other apps. NumPad never collects what you type — the keyboard records no keystrokes and contains no analytics.

The container app uses Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Performance, Remote Config) to collect anonymous, unlinked product‑interaction, crash, and performance data to improve the app. Clipboard history, if you enable Full Access, is stored only on your device (in the Keychain), expires after one hour, and is never transmitted.

Read the full policy in PrivacyPolicy.md. Questions? Email support@morevoltage.com.
